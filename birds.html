Sparrow

Sparrows are small birds with short tails and stubby beaks. They are common in cities and rural areas.

Blue Jay

Blue Jays are bright blue birds known for their intelligence and ability to mimic sounds.

Peacock

Peacocks are known for their colorful tail feathers and are native to South Asia.

Robin

Robins are small songbirds with orange chests, commonly seen in North America.
